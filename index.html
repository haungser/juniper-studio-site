About Us

At Juniper Studio, we streamline the design and construction process from start to finish. Serving as a trusted extension of your team, we ensure precision at every stage - minimizing errors, maximizing efficiency, and delivering projects that are flawlessly executed, functional, and inspiring.

With nearly 20 years of experience in the design and construction industry, specializing in kitchens, baths and interior millwork, Christina brings expertise, precision, and a collaborative spirit to every project. From conceptual space planning to detailed technical construction documentation, Christina combines creativity with technical knowledge to ensure every detail is thoughtfully executed.
